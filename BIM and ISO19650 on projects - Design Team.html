BIM and ISO 19650 on Projects - (Lead) Appointed Party - Design

BIM and ISO19650 implementation on project will include number of task that Appointed Party-Design Stage, will need to fulfill:

Planning Stage

1. Establish BIM Execution Plan based on provided Tender documents and OIR,AIR and EIR

2. Assess Capability and Capacity for the project

3. Establish Mobilisation Plan

4. Compile response to Tender

Design Stage

1. Establish TIDP/MIDP 

2. Mobilize Information Technology

3.Test Information Exchange

4. Assure adherance to BEP

5. Collaboration and Clash resolution

6. Review and Authorise Information Model

Construction Stage

1. Manage MIDP and update BEP to Construction stage

2. Prepare quality checks including data

3. Prepare Handover package in CDE - PIM

Operation and Maintenance Stage

1. Conversion of PIM to AIM if contracted

2.Update to AIM if contracted
