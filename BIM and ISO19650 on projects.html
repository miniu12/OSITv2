BIM and ISO 19650 on Projects

According to ISO19650-2, a project that requires alignment with the standard need to consider a number of steps and actions that are stakeholder sensitive. There are three main stakeholders listed in the ISO19650 standard - Appointing Party (Client), Lead Appointed Party (Tier 1 Consultant or Contractor) and Appointed Party (Sub-contractor).

ISO19650-2 is related to Delivery Phase of Assets, it contains a number of phases for any BIM aligned project. Each of phases is explained in ISO19650 guide documents including stakeholders, their responsibilities and resources needed together with required templates.

ISO19650-3 is related to Operational phase of the asset. It is quite similar to ISO19650 -2 regarding terminology and activities. Please see this guide for more information related to this phase.
