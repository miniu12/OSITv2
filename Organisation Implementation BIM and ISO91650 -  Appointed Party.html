Organisation Strategy Implementation Toolkit

Organisation Implementation - Appointed Party

The Appointed party would take number of steps to prepare their organisation for BIM/ISO19650 delivery. This includes:

- The long term goals and planning related to Digitisation and BIM - Implementation Plan.

- Required personnel training.

- Evaluate and upgrade IT infrastructure (hardware, software, telecommunication capabilities).

- Plan information exchange protocols for future project (research available CDEs).

- Prepare requested resources (i.e. standardise processes, process guides on how to structure projects, naming conventions etc.).
