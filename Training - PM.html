Education & Training - Project Management

Depending on whether a PM is representing a Client (Appointing party) or is a part of a Delivery Team, it is imperative that the Information Management process; detailed in the ISO19650 standard is clear and so are responsibilities of such individual. Additionally, understanding of personnel that delivers and coordinates BIM based projects and their tasks/responsibilities is crucial. PM can either pursue related micro credentials modules or degrees offered by number of Educational Institutions.
