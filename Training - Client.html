Education & Training - Client

Like Leadership, the Client needs to understand the possibilities and advantages that BIM and ISO19650 can offer for a project delivery. Understanding of the project information management and asset data integration are a few examples. Clients’ role in the whole process is not passive. The Information Management tasks that Client (Appointing Part (see ISO1965)) is responsible for might require training to achieve a good understanding of the process.
