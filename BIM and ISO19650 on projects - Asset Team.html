BIM and ISO 19650 on Projects - Asset team

Asset Teams (Appointed Party) are typically involved towards the end of the CAPEX phase of a project. However, the Appointing Party (Client) can involve FM/AM teams at the beginning of the project to help with detailing asset related requirements.

Asset FM teams are involved during the Operation and Maintenance (O/M) stages of a project. ISO19650-3 indicates their responsibility for projects delivered during the Operation and Management Stages.

The Appointed Party at Construction stage  will need to fulfill:

Planning Stage

1. Establish/Help Develop OIR and AIR.

O&M Stage

1 Oversee - conversion of PIM to AIM.                

2. Review and authorise updates to AIM.

3. Ensure long-term information maintenance.

4. Update asset information based on trigger events.
