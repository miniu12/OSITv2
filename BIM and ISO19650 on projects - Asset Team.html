BIM and ISO 19650 on Projects - Asset team

Asset Teams (Appointed Party) are typically involved toward the end of the CAPEX phase of a project. However, the Appointing Party (Client) can involve FM/AM teams at the beginning of the project to help with detailing asset related requirements.

Asset FM teams are involved during Operation and Maintenance (O/M)Stages of the project. ISO19650-3 indicates their responsibility for projects delivered during Operation and Management Stages.

Appointed Party at Construction stage, will need to fulfill:

Planning Stage

1. Establish OIR and AIR

O&M Stage

1 Oversee - conversion of PIM to AIM               

2. Review and authorise updates to AIM

3. Ensure long-term information maintenance

4. Update asset information based on trigger events.
