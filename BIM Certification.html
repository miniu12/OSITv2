BIM and ISO19650 Certification

Any ISO standard certification completion is evidence of the correct standard implementation withing organisation. It also applies to ISO19650 standard.

In Ireland ISO certification organisation is National Standards Authority of Ireland (NSAI). There are other organisations that provide ISO19650 certification such as BRE Group and building SMART.

Typically, there are two types of certifications on Individual/Professional and organisation level. Organisation level certification is a bit more complex and includes a number of steps and requirements that needs to be archived prior to an audit such as: organisation structure and existing processes, guides and templates. Evidence of ISO19650 implementations on project level and information management. In order to achieve such certification, organisations need to contact a body that will conduct and potentially award it. Additionally, any requirements will be also provided to an organisation interested in achieving it.
