BIM and ISO19650 Certification

Completion of the  ISO standard certifications evidence of the correct implementation of the standard withing organisation. This also applies to ISO19650 standard.

In Ireland the ISO certification organisation is the National Standards Authority of Ireland (NSAI). There are other organisations that can provide ISO19650 certification such as BRE Group and building SMART.

Typically, there are two types of certification: Individual/Professional and organisation level. Organisation level certification is a more complex and includes a number of steps and requirements that needs to be achieved prior to an audit such as: organisation structure and existing processes, guides and templates, additionally an evidence of ISO19650 implementations on a project level and information management are required. In order to achieve such certification, organisations need to contact a body that will conduct an audit and provide a certification. A list of requirements will be provided to an organisation interested in achieving this standard.
